ABOUT US

Not everyone has been blessed with a good home, quality education and a carefree childhood. While we were born in privileged families where our dreams and aspirations were supported, and opportunities as well as resources stood at our doorstep, there is a massive section of the society that does not have the basic amenities that we take for granted. These children come from slums that are sinking in poverty and illiteracy, yet the potential that they own is beyond our imagination.
Aaroha came into existence when a small group of people who believed in bringing light to these dark corners of the country decided to render their selfless services to this section of the society. They started with the aim of providing basic education, free of cost, to children of the slums and provide then with a nurturing environment in order to help their overall development.

Aaroha is an organization committed to the cause of enlightening the lives of underprivileged children and driving them from the darkness of illiteracy and ignorance.

EVENTS

AARAMBH

Aaroha made an optimistic start with Aarambh, the event was held on the 31st of July 2016 in Aashima Mall, Bhopal.

AAKAR

Aakar was a science exhibition that was put up by our children in NIT Bhopal on the 21st and 22nd of October, 2016. The underprivileged children studying in Aaroha presented their science models.

अ से अः

Aaroha is proud to have organized another successful even on the 12th of February 2017 at Shree Bhavan’s Bharti School. The event was aimed at making our children feel special.

PHOTO WALK

The children were taken for a visit to the Tribal Museum. This place gives a gist of ancient lifestyle of the people.

FUNDRAISING AT UPPER LAKE

On the 26th of January, while the country was busy celebrating the Republic Day of India, the volunteers at Aaroha took this opportunity to raise funds at Upper Lake

‘AASHA’ – A ray of hope
